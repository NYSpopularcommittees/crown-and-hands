Welcome

New York State was built by workers, neighbors, farmers, and tenants — not by the rich. Now it's time to organize block by block, county by county, and build people’s power everywhere.

Join or start a local committee. Read our story. Take action in your workplace, your building, your town.

About This Project

The Crown & The Hands is a project to rebuild working-class power in New York. We tell our true history and offer real steps for organizing today — in every part of the state, from the Bronx to Buffalo, from the farms to the factories.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Talk to your neighbors, co-workers, or tenants.
  2. Set up a meeting in your building, block, or workplace.
  3. Identify the issues affecting your community.
  4. Make a plan to address them together.
  5. Link up with other committees in your area and across the state.
